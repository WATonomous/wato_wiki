# Finance System

Instructions on how to use the [WATonomous Finance System](finance-frontend.watonomous.ca), which is used to track funding, our account balances, and manage and reimburse purhcases.

`Creating and Submitting a Personal Purchase Request` Contains details for members on how to purchase items using their own money and get reimbursed for it. ([go-to])(/finance/creating_personal_purchases)

`Creating and Submitting a Purchase Request` Contains details for members on how to purchase items using WATonomous cash or directly from one of our funds. ([go-to])(/finance/creating_purchase_requests)
