## How to Edit

Editing the WATonomous wiki is easy. Simply click `Edit this page` on the far right of each page. Note, you must be part of the WATonomous Organization to be able to edit.

More how-to-edit [here](https://github.com/WATonomous/wato_wiki?tab=readme-ov-file#editing-the-wiki). You can use regular react components, or choose from a plethora of pre-built components and tools [here](https://nextra.site/docs).
